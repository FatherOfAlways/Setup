## Troubleshooting

*The overlay is showing me 0.0 FPS*

- There is no running process detected or there are multiple processes detected and you haven't chosen one of them. Go to Capture > Running Processes and move any undesirable processes to Ignore list.


*Importing Templates*

- Templates are stored under ~\Documents\CapFrameX\Configuration.
